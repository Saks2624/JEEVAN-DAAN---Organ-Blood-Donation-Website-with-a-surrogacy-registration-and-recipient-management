Frequently Asked Questions

Is surrogacy legal in India?

Yes, under strict legal guidelines and for altruistic (non-commercial) purposes only.

Can the surrogate keep the baby?

No. Legal contracts ensure parental rights are with the intended parents.

What support is provided to surrogates?

Medical, emotional, legal, and financial coverage is provided as per the policy.
