Who can Donate?

Almost anyone can become a donor. Organ donation is not limited by age or health status. Many people have donated organs in their 70s or even 80s, proving that age is not a limitation for becoming a donor. It’s the condition of the organs that matters most.

Learn More

Why Donate?

Organ donation is a selfless gift that saves lives. Every year, thousands of people die waiting for a transplant due to a shortage of organ donors. Becoming a donor gives patients in need of a transplant a second chance at life.

Learn More

How to Register?

Registering as an organ donor is simple. You can register online, at a hospital, or through your local health department. Start the process today and make a difference.
